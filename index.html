About Me

I graduated in 2003 with a Bachelor of Science in the Music Recording Industry program at MTSU, and went on to produce and engineer songs for myself, other songwriters, and bands. I composed, produced, and engineered original music for 2 films which were entered into film festivals in Nashville TN and Atlanta GA.

My interest in technology led me to an IT position at a Credit Union, where I worked from 2009-2016. After much deliberation, I left my IT job to attend Nashville Software School, a 6 month JavaScript and Python coding bootcamp. I thrive on creativity. Coding has provided me with a perfect balance of everything that inspires me: creativity and technology. I am also an avid painter and visual artist, and run an online art store where I sell my art, EphemeralWave.com. I am happily employed, and continue to learn new frameworks, languages, and ideas.

Angular / WebAudio API / WebMIDI API
eWave Studio

AngularJS app created for my front-end "Capstone" project at NSS.

For this app, I wanted to create an audio effects processor that I could easily use in my home studio. My initial goal was to create an audio looper that would record audio and loop it so I could layer sounds. Because of the endless possibilities with the Web Audio API, I was able to achieve many of my stretch goals including MIDI controllers and a drum machine / sequencer.

Users are able to plug an instrument into their computer via USB inputs, add effects processors, record audio, loop recorded audio, and add recorded audio to a sequencer/drum machine. Users can plug MIDI devices to trigger start/stop recording, and access a MIDI sampler / synth.
